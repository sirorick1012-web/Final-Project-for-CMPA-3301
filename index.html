The Start Of Active Directory

Here we will begin with what steps are needed.

  • A. Creating user and group management.
  • B. Group Policy Objects (GPOs) for security.
  • C. Creation of Objects for devices (computers, laptops, ect).
    • 1. Install Active Directory management on your pc.
    • 2. Open Active Directory and find your domain.
    • 3. Create folders based on the department names.
    • 4. Start creating your security groups based on roles and needs.
    • 5. Create your objects in the respective folders.

Now, install the group policy management on your device. Once installed, open your Group Policy Management console. Here you can either

  • 1. Create policy from scratch and attach to the folders you need it on;
    • or
  • 2. Attach an existing policy to the folders needed.
